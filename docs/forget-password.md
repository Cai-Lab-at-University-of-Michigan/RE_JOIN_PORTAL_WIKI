*This tutorial explains how to retrieve a forgotten password.*

---

## Forgot password?

Go to the login page:  
https://rejon.cai-lab.org/login

Steps:

1. Click `Forgot Password?`
2. Enter the `email` associated with your account.
3. A password recovery email will be sent to the registered address.

---

### Troubleshooting
- If you don't receive the email, check your spam/junk folder.  
- If you still don't receive it, confirm you used the correct email or contact the site administrator for help.
